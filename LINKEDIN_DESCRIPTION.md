# Tacit - LinkedIn Business Page Description

**Tacit - Multiply Your Leadership Impact**

Turn your tacit knowledge into organizational leverage.

**The Challenge:**
As a leader, your expertise lives in your head - the context behind decisions, strategic thinking, and judgment calls that drive results. When you're unavailable, work stalls. Your team waits. Opportunities slip.

**The Solution:**
Tacit is your AI-powered work twin that captures, organizes, and shares your decision-making patterns so your team can move forward with confidence - even when you're not in the room.

**How It Works:**
• **Capture Context** - Document decisions, strategies, meeting insights, and plans as you work
• **Intelligent Search** - Upload documents and get instant answers from your knowledge base
• **Executive Coaching** - Think through leadership challenges while your twin learns your patterns
• **Team Enablement** - Give your team access to your thinking when they need it most

**Built For:**
VPs, Directors, and executives who need to scale their impact without becoming a bottleneck.

**The Result:**
Your team makes better decisions faster. You spend less time answering "what did we decide about X?" and more time driving strategy forward.

Stop being the bottleneck. Start being everywhere.

**Technology:**
Powered by Claude AI with enterprise-grade semantic search and document intelligence.
