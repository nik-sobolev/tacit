Three steps.

/ how it works
01 / DROP

Paste a link

Any URL goes in. Tacit fetches it, transcribes video and audio, writes a clean summary and tags, then sets it down as a card.

02 / CONNECT

Watch it link itself

Cards draw threads to each other by meaning, not by tags you maintain. The relationships between ideas become something you can see.

03 / ASK

Talk to all of it

Ask a question and Tacit searches everything you've saved, answers in plain language, and shows you the exact source card.

A memory that does the organizing for you.

/ features
CONVERSATIONAL RECALL

Chat with your whole canvas

Ask a question in plain language. Tacit searches across everything you've ever saved, answers, and links you straight back to the card it came from — with the receipts.

PEOPLE

Remembers who's who

Mention a person and Tacit keeps track, surfacing every place they've come up.

SEARCH

Find it as you type

Real-time search by tag, title, or full content. The canvas filters live.

NOTES

Your own cards too

Write notes that live on the canvas and connect to everything else automatically.

EVERYWHERE

Saves from wherever you are

Runs in any browser, installs as a PWA on your iPhone, and ships an iOS Shortcut so you can share a URL from Safari straight onto your canvas.
